The files in this directory are the raw output from the Dsuite Dtrios program (https://github.com/millanek/Dsuite). The species in the file name indicates the outgroup for that set of results. Different outgroups were used to confirm that choice of outgroup did not influence the results. The following trees were tested:
((((((S. droebachiensis, S. fragilis), S. pallidus), S. intermedius), S. purpuratus), H. pulcherrimus), P. depressus);
((((((S. droebachiensis, S. fragilis), S. pallidus), S. intermedius), S. purpuratus), H. pulcherrimus), M. franciscanus);
((((((S. droebachiensis, S. fragilis), S. pallidus), S. intermedius), S. purpuratus), H. pulcherrimus), M. nudus);
(((M. nudus, M. franciscanus), P. depressus), S. fragilis);
(((M. nudus, M. franciscanus), P. depressus), S. purpuratus);
